Welcome to the Sion-Dharavi Road Footpath Survey

This project aims to assess the conditions of footpaths on the Sion-Dharavi Road, ensuring they are safe, accessible, and pedestrian-friendly. Through detailed surveys, audits, and community feedback, we aim to highlight the current state of the footpaths and work towards improvements.

Survey & Audit Checklist

What to look for?

  • Broken footpath
  • Illegally tall footpath (>15cm)
  • Illegally parked vehicle
  • Bad bus shelter design
  • Illegally narrow bollards (<1m)
  • A short pedestrian signal timer
  • Litter on the footpath
  • Un-sloped footpath kerb
  • Tactile paving on the footpath
  • Poor tree cover
  • Obstacle blocking the walkway
  • Faded zebra crossing
  • Wide footpath (>3m)
  • Footpath encroachment
